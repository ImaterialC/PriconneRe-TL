##Profile##
【魔法】後衛で、難しい魔法の練習に励む海辺の優等生。ランダムな敵に当たるスキルの威力は攻撃の際に付与する【バタフライシール】で増幅し、防御力もダウンさせる。=[Magic] A seaside honor student who works hard to practice difficult magic as a RG. The power of skills that hit random enemies is amplified by the [Butterfly Seal] that is given when attacking, and the M.DEF is also reduced.
