##Profile##
【物理】前衛で、二人きりの月下の空旅にときめく黒猫。物防弱体の他、ダメージ100ヒット後には華麗なステップの追撃とＴＰ回復量増加で、砂漠の夜空に花を咲かせる。=[Physical] Frontline black cat thrilled by a moonlit sky journey just for two. In addition to debuffing P.DEF, after 100 damage hits, performs a splendid step follow-up attack and increases TP recovery, making flowers bloom in the desert night sky.
