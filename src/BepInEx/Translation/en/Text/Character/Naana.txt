##Profile##
【物理】前衛でダメダメでも逃げず戦うアルミラージ娘。最も近くの味方に強化や行動不能無効の付与、回復を行い敵の注意をそらすことで、過酷な世界を生き延びる。=[Physical] A frontline Almiraj girl who fights without fleeing even when she's down and out. She buffs the nearest ally with enhancements and disable immunity, provides recovery, and draws enemy attention to survive in a harsh world.
