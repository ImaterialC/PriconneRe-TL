##Profile##
【魔法】後衛で絵馬に込めた想いを胸に抱く晴れ着の妹。クリティカル時に威力が上がる矢で敵を撃ち抜き、味方全体への攻撃支援や継続ＴＰ回復で厄を払い福を届ける。=[Magic] A kimono-clad younger sister in the back line, holding close the wishes written on her ema plaques. She pierces enemies with arrows that grow stronger on critical hits, while driving away misfortune and delivering blessings through attack support for all allies and continuous TP recovery.
