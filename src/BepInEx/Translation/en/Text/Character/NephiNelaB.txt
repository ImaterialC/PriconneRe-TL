##Profile##
【物理】中衛から怯懦を退け、万魔を率する煉獄の鉄媛。千変万化の躰は行動速度アップを始めとする多彩な支援とすべての敵の弱体を両立し、天地万象を掌の内に収める。=[Physical] A hellforged iron princess who drives away cowardice and commands countless fiends from the midline. Her ever-changing form balances a wide array of support—led by action speed boosts—with weakening all enemies, grasping all creation in the palm of her hand.
