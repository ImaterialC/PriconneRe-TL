##Profile##
【物理】中衛で眷属と力を合わせて戦う誇り高き蛇乙女。【魔眼】の数に応じて効果量が上がるスキルのダメージと弱体化で範囲内の敵を追い詰め、鋭い瞳で石へと変える。=[Physical] A proud snake maiden who combines strength with her servants. Her skills increase based on the number of [Evil Eye] stacks she has, and using debuffs to make surrounding enemies weaker, she uses her sharp eyes to turn her foes into stone.
