##Profile##
【魔法】中衛で、騎士の隣に一歩踏み出す聖夜の魔法士。敵の注目を集めながら攻防支援を行い【聖夜の愛慕】が高まると、ＴＰ支援やダメージカットで優しく包み込む。=[Magic] A midline magic caster who steps forward beside Kishi on this holy night. She draws enemy attention while providing offensive and defensive support, and when [Holy Night Adoration] builds up, she gently envelops allies with TP support and damage cuts.
