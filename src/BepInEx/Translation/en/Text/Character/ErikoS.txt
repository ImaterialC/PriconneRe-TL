##Profile##
【物理】中衛で、火花を纏い夕闇を彩る真夏の壊し屋。二つのスキルで物理防御力を下げ、ダメージ量に応じてクリティカル上昇が延長するＵＢで眼前の敵を粉砕する。=[Physical] A midsummer Destroyer that colors the evening with sparks in midlines. She uses two skills to lower her physical defense and crush enemies in front of her with a UB that extends critical boosts based on the amount of damage she takes.
