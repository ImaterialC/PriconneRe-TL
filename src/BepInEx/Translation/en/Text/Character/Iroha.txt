##Profile##
【物理】中衛で、大きな体で無邪気に遊び回る天才剣士。戦いの中で【剣圧】を溜め、その後のスキルやＵＢで物防一定無視のダメージを連発し、才気煥発の活躍を見せる。=[Physical] A genius swordsman who plays around in a big body with child-like innocence.
During combat she gains [Sword Pressure], which allows her to continuously dish out damage that ignores physical defense, allowing her radiant genius to shine through.
