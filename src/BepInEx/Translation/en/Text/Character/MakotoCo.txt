##Profile##
【物理】前衛で、情と熱意で仲間を束ねる指揮官の狼娘。敵の攻守を削りつつ、自分のＴＰ回復や対象が物防ダウン状態の時に発生する追撃で猛攻を仕掛け前線を突破する。=[Physical] A wolfgirl commander who uses feelings and passion to rally her allies. She chips away at the enemy's attack and defense, boosts her own TP and has special bonus attacks that trigger when enemy's physical defense is lowered, breaking through the enemy lines.
