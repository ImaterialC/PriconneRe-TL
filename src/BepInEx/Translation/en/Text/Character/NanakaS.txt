##Profile##
【魔法】中衛の多勢相手の戦いが得意な真夏の魔法少女。敵の数に応じて効果が上昇する防御力ダウンのスキルや、多彩な範囲攻撃を操り、真夏の戦いをサイカワに制する。=[Magic] A midsummer Magical Girl who is good at fighting against large numbers of midliners. Using her skill to lower magical defense, which increases in effect depending on the number of enemies, and with variety of ranged attacks, Saikawa wins the midsummer battle.
