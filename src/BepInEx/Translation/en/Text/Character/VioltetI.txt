##Profile##
【物理】前衛から慈愛の心で冥府を包む彼岸の屍姫。ＵＢは敵全体を呑み込む割合ダメージを与え、【冥海の加護】を消費して味方の攻防ダウン効果までも禊ぎ清める。=[Physical] A corpse princess of the nether shore who envelops the underworld with compassion from the front line.Her UB deals proportional damage that engulfs all enemies, and by consuming 【Underworld Blessing】, purifies even P.ATK, M.ATK, P.DEF, and M.DEF down effects affecting allies.
