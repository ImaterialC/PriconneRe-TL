##Profile##
【物理】前衛で、光を操り味方を守る聖夜のサレンママ。敵の攻撃を防ぎ、ダメージを与えるユニオンバーストや、ダメージを与えつつ回復するスキルで、攻守双方を担う。=[Physical] A frontline fighter who wields the power of light and protects allies, Mama Saren is the saint of the night. With UB that both defends against enemy attacks and deals damage, as well as skills that deal damage while also restoring health, she is a versatile attacker and defender.
