##Profile##
【物理】前衛で、堅牢に仲間を守る海辺のお姉ちゃん。物理防御力が高い程、効果が高まる複数のスキルを巧みに操り防御と補助役をしつつ範囲攻撃で攻撃役をもこなす。=[Physics] A vanguard, sturdy seaside Big Sis who protects her allies. She skillfully uses multiple skills that increase in effectiveness as her physical defense increases to act as a defensive and supportive role while also performing attacks with area-of-effect damage.
