##Profile##
【魔法】後衛で、主さまを想い“進化”した嚮導の巫女。極大ダメージのＵＢで敵を穿ち、【進化超越】の権能によ って、味方のバフやダメージを、限界を超え成長させる。=[Magic] A rearguard shrine maiden who has "evolved" out of longing for her master. Pierces foes with extreme damage UB, and with the authority of [Evolution Transcendence], pushes ally buffs and damage beyond their limits to new heights.
