##Profile##
【魔法】後衛から、仲間を啓蒙するユニちゃんズの才媛。自分以外の味方全員の物理・魔法両方の攻撃力アップとクリティカル時のダメージアップで皆を勝利へと導く。=[Magic] A genius member of the Yuni-chanzu who enlightens her allies from the rear guard. She leads everyone to victory with her skills that boost both P.ATK & M.ATK for all allies except herself, and also boost damage dealt when landing critical hits.
